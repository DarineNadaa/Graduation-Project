Co-creation

over competition

Tackling and solving digital challenges in a community of creative freelancers. A place where everyone contributes their own expertise and can pursue their passion.

Co-creation comes first for us, and that is no coincidence. Our statutes say the cooperative's purpose is to "facilitate projects, including project management, events, community, co-creation and knowledge sharing."

01

Storytelling

as inspiration

A stage for every story. Whether you are a company, an artist or an individual, we create a digital stage that fits your unique story.

Brands without a story do not need a stage. That is why we believe in everything connected to storytelling. Your voice is what makes you distinct.

02

Equality

Over autocracy

As the cooperative keeps evolving, the focus stays on delivering value for both our clients and creative freelancers.

Our statutes make equality concrete: "All members have equal rights and obligations within the cooperative, including equal voting rights."

Members also have indirect influence over the composition of the board. During general members' meetings, board members are appointed by all members.

Every member, regardless of background or position within the cooperative, has the same voting right in decisions within the collective.

03

Transparency

In every process

Profit sharing, client communication and invoicing. We are open and honest at every step.

Our profit is not divided among the board or founders. It is invested in software, tools, workshops, events and creative sessions.

Every member can submit ideas for investment, and then all members vote together on what to invest in. You are not working for the boss's third Ferrari.

04

Curiosity

To share, grow and discover

In the client. In technology. We believe today's challenges can become tomorrow's knowledge. As pioneers in theno/low-code movement in the Netherlandswe are just as excited about new developments as you are.

It fits perfectly with the cooperative's mission, vision and goals. And if you see new opportunities for innovation, the cooperative's profit is available for them.
